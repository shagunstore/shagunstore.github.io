Our Story

We opened this store in 1776 because we believed in America, and the power of freedom. Freedom to buy what you wanted, when you wanted, wherever you wanted.

When I was 7 years old, I wanted to listen to Blurred Lines, but my mother wouldn't let me. Since then, I've been on a mission to connect people with music.
